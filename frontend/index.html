Empowering Communities: A Decade of Service

IIIT-NR Unit sets new benchmark in rural development and social welfare through student-led initiatives.

Fig 1.1: Volunteers engaged in community service.

The National Service Scheme (NSS) unit at IIIT-Naya Raipur has once again demonstrated its unwavering commitment to social upliftment. With a fresh batch of volunteers joining the ranks this semester, the unit is poised to expand its footprint in the adopted villages of Naya Raipur.

From digital literacy campaigns to health awareness drives, the students have been at the forefront of change. "Not Me, But You" is not just a motto; it is the lived reality of every volunteer who dedicates their weekends to community service.

About The NSS Unit

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports.

It provides opportunity to the student youth of 11th & 12th Class of schools at +2 Board level and student youth of Technical Institution, Graduate & Post Graduate at colleges and University level of India to take part in various government led community service activities & programmes to make a difference in the society.

Motto: "Not Me, But You"
